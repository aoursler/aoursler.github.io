PlayGnd

The PlayGnd TechVest was my first big, or should I say small, hardware project. It was done as part of a wonderful team of five for the final project of Comp50WD. Our goal was to encourage children to engage in imaginitve and inclusive outdoor play. Through this project I got to learn about rfid readers, individualy addressable led strips, homemade flex sensors, connecting multiple aduinos, designing pcbs and much much more.
